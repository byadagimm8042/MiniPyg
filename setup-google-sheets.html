Step 1: Create Google Sheet

Click the button below to create a new Google Sheet:

Create New Google Sheet

After creating, name it "MiniPyg Form Submissions"

Step 2: Get Spreadsheet ID

In your Google Sheet, look at the URL in your browser:

https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/SPREADSHEET_ID_HERE/edit

Copy the part between /d/ and /edit - that's your Spreadsheet ID

Step 3: Create Google Apps Script

Click the button below to open Google Apps Script:

Open Google Apps Script
  1. Click "New Project"
  2. Delete the default code
  3. Copy the code from google-apps-script.js in your project
  4. Replace YOUR_SPREADSHEET_ID_HERE with your actual Spreadsheet ID
  5. Click Save (üíæ) and name it "MiniPyg Form Handler"
